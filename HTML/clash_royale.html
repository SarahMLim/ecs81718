Clash Royale

My rating:


Summary of Game

Clash Royale is a fun strategy game where the goal is to destroy your opponent's towers. Each player has 3 towers, two arena towers and one king tower. A player wins by eliminating more of their opponent's towers by the time the game ends. A player can also win by destroying their opponent's king tower which immediately ends the game. You do this by placing out "cards" into the arena that battle each other and do damage to the towers. All of the cards in the game do different things and are good to use in different situations. The cards also cost varying amounts of exiler to use, which you get over time as you battle. This game involves a lot of strategy because you get to design your own deck of 8 cards from your collection to battle with.


Quick Facts
Release date January 4, 2016
Platform Android and iOS
Genre Player vs. Player
Cost Free with in app purchases
Developer Supercell

Game Review

Overall I gave this game 4.5 stars. I love how much strategy there is involved in creating and playing with your deck and how you get to play with other real people. I also really like being able to join clans, which are groups of players that can do friendly battles and donate cards to each other. The only thing that I do not like about this game is that in order to progress more quickly you have to buy in-app purchases. This makes it slightly unfair for people who are not willing to spend money on the game.
