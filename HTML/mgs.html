Metal Gear Solid

My rating:


Summary of the franchise

The Metal Gear Franchise is a series of games that involve you sneaking through levels using stealth mechanics to succeed. You usually work with the government to stop a machine that plans to start a war by nuking the country. The game was very linear until the last installment beacause the game was made open world and your choices affect the world. The story is kind of confusing because chronologically the 3rd game is first, the 4th one is last and all the other games are in between.


Quick Facts
First release date 1998
Platform PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows, OSX
Genre Action adventure, Stealth
Cost at release $59.99
Developer Konami

Game Review

Overall I gave this franchise 5 stars. I gave this franchise 5 stars because the gameplay is very good and the story is also pretty good. What I like about this game is that gameplay is just right. It is not too hard and not too easy. I liked the story because it is not too simple so it makes you think about it. After you understand the story the whole game makes sense and the gameplay experience is better. The only bad things about this franchise is that the creator made the boss battles hard because the only way to defeat the boss is to use machine guns and rocket launchers. Even though it is expensive, this game is definitely worth the $59.99 you have to pay.
