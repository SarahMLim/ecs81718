The Legend of Zelda

My rating:


Summary of the franchise

The Zelda franchise is about a hero who is reincarnated to fight the reincarnated faces of evil. The name of that hero is Link and the evil beast is Ganandorf. Ganandorf has a plan to take over the Triforce which is a sacred relic that the goddeses left behind when they created earth. One of the goddesses also reincarnates as Zelda a princess who is always abducted by Ganandorf. So Link goes on a journey to save princess Zelda and Hyrule from the forces of evil. On his journey he makes valuable friends and allies who help him save the world.


Quick Facts
First release date 1987
Platform NES, SNES, N64, GAMECUBE, WII, WII U, NINTENDO SWITCH, GAMEBOY COLOR, GAMEBOY ADVANCED, NINTENDO DS, NINTENDO 3DS
Genre Action adventure
Cost at release $59.99
Developer Nintendo

Game Review

Overall I gave this franchise 5 stars because the story is really good. The story is not too hard to understand and not too easy to understand. It is in a way that makes you think but doesn't challenge you. When it comes to gameplay the boss battles can be hard but there is always a trick that helps you defeat them. Excluding the boss battles the gameplay is good because there is always something new like every game has at least one weapon that is incredibly powerful and this makes the game fun. The only flaw that the game has is that the story can sometimes be cliche because in it Zelda is always abucted by Ganandorf and Link goes on a journey to save her. It can get kind of repetitive and boring. Aside from that the franchise is very good and I would reccommend it to anyone that was looking for a good time.
