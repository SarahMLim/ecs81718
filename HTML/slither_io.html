Slither.io

My rating:


Summary of Game

Slither.io is a fun and intense multiplayer game where the object is to grow your snake to be as long as possible. You can do this by eating colorful dots that spawn on the large circular map or killing other snakes and eating the dots that they drop. You can kill another snake by causing their head to run into the side of your snake. You can also use a speed boost to help you kill another snake or to run away from another snake but this uses up your points and slowly makes you smaller. If you die by having your head run into another snake, you lose all of your points and have to start over.


Quick Facts
Release date March 25, 2016
Platform Android, iOS, and web browser
Genre Massive Multiplayer
Cost Free but has advertisements
Developer Steve Howse

Game Review

Overall I gave this game 3 stars. I love how fast and action-packed it is and how much fun it is to have a giant snake. I think that it was a creative idea and has well thought out mechanics. The only thing that I don't like about this game is how frustrating it is when you die and have to start over. I think this game would be better if you at least got to keep half of your points like some other .io games that I have played. Despite being frustrating this game is still one of my favorite mobile/browser games.
