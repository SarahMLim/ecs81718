Malala Yousafzai

Mezon Yousafzai

Malala Yousafzai is a Pakistani activist for female education and openly spoke out against the Taliban's plans to limit female education.

She advocated for equal education opportunities because she experienced discrimination for going to school just because she was female.

Through her speeches and activism, she inspired many girls to stand up for themselves and to believe that they are just as equal as boys in their right to an education.


Malala Yousafzai Wikipedia
  • Wrote a book called 'I Am Malala' to promote her belief of equal education
  • Won a Nobel Peace Prize for all her hard work in advocating for education for girls
  • Sparked worlwide discussion about children's education
  • Influenced the UN to recommit to Millennium Development Goal 2 (which encourages universal and equal education opportunities)
  • Had three million people sign the Malala Petition, a petition created by her to require primary education for children around the world.
