Drive Ahead

My rating:


Summary of Game

Drive Ahead is a fast-paced 2D physics based driving game where the object is to destroy your opponent by running into their head with your car. You also have to avoid a variety of obstacles from giant saws to raining penguins. The first player to win 5 rounds wins the game. The only controls in the game are the forward button and the backward button. There are many different interesting maps that you can play in as well as many different cars that you can use. This game allows you to play singleplayer against and AI opponent or multiplayer through wifi or two players on one device.


Quick Facts
Release date 2015
Platform Android and iOS
Genre Driving game
Cost Free with in-app purchases
Developer Dodreams

Game Review

Overall I gave this game a rating of 4 stars. I love how exciting and challenging it is to hit your opponent's head while also protecting yours. I espiecially like the multiplayer mode where you can play with your friends using the same device. The singleplayer missions where you fight bosses are also very fun. The only thing that I do not like about this game is that it takes a long time to get coins to buy new vehicles and maps, but once you do get them they add a lot to the game.
