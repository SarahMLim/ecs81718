Minecraft

My rating:


Summary of Game

Minecraft is a sandbox game where the world is made out of blocks. You can place and destroy blocks to create whatever you want. You can choose to play in creative mode, where you have unlimited resources, can fly, and are invincible, or you can play on surival mode where you have to collect resources by mining and cutting down trees and you have to battle monsters to survive. The two different modes of minecraft are almost two different games becuase in creative the only objective is to build things and in survival the objective is to stay alive.


Quick Facts
Release date May 17. 2009
Platform Windows, OSX, iOS, Android, Xbox 360, Xbox One, PS3, PS4
Genre Survival, sandbox
Cost $26.95
Developer Mojang

Game Review

I gave this game 4 stars. I mostly enjoy the creative mode of the game because I got bored of survival very quickly. It is very frustrating when you die and lose all of your stuff. The best part of Minecraft is being able to build whatever you want. You can even make electronics like calculators with a block called redstone, which can be used to make logic gates. I learned a lot about how computers work just from building redstone contraptions in Minecraft. I don't play this game very much anymore (you have to get tired of a game after playing it for 3 years straight) but it is still one of my favorites.
