Elephants are some of the largest creatures in the world. They can live for 50 - 70 years. They are also known to be extremely intelligent. For the most part elephants are gentle animals but they will attack if they feel themselves or their offspring are in danger. Elephants eat grass, small plants, bushes, fruit, twigs, tree bark, and roots.
