Terraria

My rating:


Summary of Game

Terraria is a 2D action adventure/sandbox game that is similar to Minecraft. When the player starts the game they spawn in a randomly generated world made of blocks with some basic tools. Over time the player can get more and more advanced tools, weapons, and armor by mining ore, battling monsters, and defeating bosses. Many resources can only be found in certain areas of the map, so the player has to explore to find them. By completing certain goals and building houses the player can also attract NPCs to move in which sell useful items. Terraria is the type of game where you are never really finished because you can always keep building and exploring.


Quick Facts
Release date May 16, 2011
Platform Windows, OSX, iOS, Android, Xbox 360, Xbox One, PS3, PS4
Genre Survival, sandbox
Cost $9.99
Developer Re-Logic

Game Review

This game definitely gets 5 stars. The thing that I like so much about this game that isn't in most of the other games is how creative you can be. You can really make whatever you want from a village to bridge across the sky. This game is similar to Minecraft but I like it better because of the larger variety of things you can do. There are so many different things in Terraria, from interesting monsters to cool looking furniture. When you play Terraria you can do whatever you are in the mood for, whether it be building houses, exploring caves, or taking on challenging bosses. I can't think of anything I don't like about this game.
