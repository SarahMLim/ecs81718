Assassin's Creed

Our rating:


Summary of the franchise

Assassin's Creed is about a person who has no name (they have codenames like subject 17) and they work for a company called Abstergo. This company makes videogames but in reality they are Templars. Templars are a group that try to take over the world so that they can make peace. So you (the player) use virtual reality to go back in time to replay a specific time in history. In the game you play as a specific assassin (assassins are a group of poeple who oppose the templars and try to make a democratic goverment) who is looking for a specififc part of eden (eden is an ancient artifact that is shaped like an apple and has tremendous power).


Quick Facts
First release date 2007
Platform PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows, OSX, PSP, PS VITA
Genre Action adventure, Stealth
Cost at release $59.99
Developer Ubisoft

Game Review

Overall I gave this franchise 4 stars because of 1 release. What I liked about the game is that it is a stealth game. I am a fan of stealth games. Another thing that I liked is the story. The story is not complex and some games like Assassin's Creed 2 have a really good plot. Also the gameplay is almost always new because all gameplay is the same up to Assassin's Creed IV were the gameplay changes in the way that you now command a ship and you are a pirate. The only reason this game got a 4 is because of Assassin's Creed Revelations. I bought that game and I have not finished it because the gameplay is not improved and the story is not very exiting. Also the game is not very creative because all you're doing is looking for keys to open a door which contains a piece of eden.
