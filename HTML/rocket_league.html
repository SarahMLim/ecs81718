Rocket League

My rating:


Summary of Game

Rocket League is literaly soccer in a rocket powered car. The object of the game is to help your team score the most goals with the giant soccer ball by the end of the game. You play with other real people in online matches and you have the option of choosing how many people are on each team. You can also play in singleplayer matches againt AI but it isn't as fun. Since the cars are rocket powered, you can fly by using your boost energy that you get by running over boost spots. This game can be very difficult for beginners but it gets more fun as you get better.


Quick Facts
Release date July 7, 2015
Platform Windows, OSX, Linux, PS4, Xbox One
Genre Driving game
Cost $19.99 with in-app purchases
Developer Psyonix

Game Review

I gave this game 4.5 out of 5 stars. Although it sounds like a strange game it is so much fun to drive around in a rocket powered car while trying to hit a gaint soccer ball. I really like how much teamwork is involved in playing with other real people to try to win the game. The only problem with the game being multiplayer is that it is often very laggy. You might freeze up when you are about to score or glitch back to where you were a few seconds ago. Even with this problem Rocket League is definitly one of my top games.
